# HabitQuest - Player Guide & Rules

This document contains the specific win conditions and rules for playing HabitQuest.

## March 2026 (Weeks 10-14)

**Monthly Theme:** Vitality (Eating) - All XP gains from the Vitality habit are doubled!

---

### Habit Win Conditions

#### 1. Vitality (VIT, Eating - Priority 10/10)
*   **Goal:** Follow the eating code prescribed by your nutritionist.
*   **Small Win:** Record your meals for the day.
*   **Mid Win:** Do not have a night snack.
*   **Big Win:** Fully comply with all of your nutritionist's guidance for the day.

#### 2. Agility (AGI, Career - Priority 9/10)
*   **Goal:** Take action to advance your career.
*   **Small Win:** Send a cold message (to an outside contact) or a warm message (to an internal contact).
*   **Mid Win:** Spend time on career development activities like updating your CV or watching relevant YouTube videos.
*   **Big Win:** Conduct an interview simulation.

#### 3. Strength (STR, Workout - Priority 8/10)
*   **Goal:** Perform a physical activity (Running, swimming, etc.).
*   **Small Win:** 1 minute of activity.
*   **Mid Win:** 5 minutes of activity.
*   **Big Win:** 10 minutes of activity.

#### 4. Will Power (WIL, Getup - Priority 7/10)
*   **Goal:** Get out of bed on time, starting with a target of 08:00.
*   **Small Win:** Get up within 10 minutes of your target time.
*   **Mid Win:** Get up within 5 minutes of your target time.
*   **Big Win:** Get up at or before your target time.
    *(Note: The original rules had 1, 5, 10 min, which seems to overlap. I have adjusted to reflect getting closer to the goal.)*

#### 5. Intelligence (INT, Learning - Priority 6/10)
*   **Goal:** Engage with a learning course or book.
*   **Small Win:** 5 minutes of listening or reading.
*   **Mid Win:** 10 minutes of listening or reading.
*   **Big Win:** 30 minutes of listening/reading, AND write down 10 words on your X account, OR create a podcast on LinkedIn and Substack.
